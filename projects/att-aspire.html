AT&T Inspire

Connecting to Audiences through Value-Rich Communications

Since 2008, AT&T has committed its assets, influence and expertise to help ensure all students have an equal shot at a quality education. Called AT&T Aspire, the $350 million signature education initiative aims to transform learning. With the goal of positioning AT&T as a technology and social innovation leader, AT&T Aspire engaged GOODcorps to develop a strategic communications plan.

Leveraging Aspire’s existing programmatic and communications assets, GOODcorps collaborated with the Aspire team to deliver meaningful value to key Aspire audiences and reflect AT&T’s leadership in Aspire’s strategic execution.

What did GOOD did

Our 9-week sprint consisted of a 4-phased approach that sought to address the following foundational:

  • Given AT&T's top business and social impact goals for Aspire and the existing Aspire campaign assets, what are strategic opportunity areas to assume a genuine leadership position in the social innovation and ed tech space?
  • Which audience groups can we engage? (e.g. further segmenting the Beltway elite audience or identifying new groups) How might Aspire respond to their needs and interests?
  • What is the CTA that engages AT&T's target audience in the Aspire work? To what end?
  • How might AT&T best leverage its existing creative assets throughout 2015-2016 to support this goal?

Through our agile and swift process: we conducted a full audit of AT&T Aspire’s messaging strategy and creative content, held conversations with key internal stakeholders. pulled inspiration from the world of values-driven corporate leadership and communication models, synthesized our findings, engaged in quick ideation and communication approaches, and developed recommendation for the Aspire team. The final deliverable was an audience strategy and messaging playbook.

What GOODcorps Did

Building off of Aspire’s previous success and current pivot towards innovative philanthropy targeting the Ed-Tech community, GOODcorps worked through a process to 1) prioritize key audience groups for broader public engagement given the immediate value Aspire’s current programming provided 2) surface strategic opportunities with each audience group 3) define communications goals 4) identify immediate and long term steps towards action. Additionally, GOODcorps provided an Aspire Messaging Companion Playbook outlining tactical updates to the current messaging strategy as well as outlining key inspirational approaches from the landscape of social good.

Highlights

  • 310,000+ social reach
  • 650,000+ video views
  • 10MM+ banner impressions
  • Built a new community of partnerships cultivated from years of relationship building in the education landscape
  • Top callout 2: Design with Value in Mind- When seeking sustained engagement, focus on providing value to the lives of key stakeholders. In this case, AT&T sought to understand and address the needs of core influencers, beneficiaries and collaborators within the ed-tech and social impact space.
